Part 3: Trace Element Proxies

The Dilution Problem

Sediments contain variable proportions of carbonate, opal, organic matter, and detrital material. Raw trace element concentrations are not directly comparable between samples.

‚úì Solution: Normalize to Aluminum

Aluminum is:

  • Hosted in aluminosilicates (clays)
  • Representative of detrital fraction
  • Immobile during diagenesis

Normalizing to Al removes dilution effects and isolates authigenic enrichment.

Enrichment Factor (EF)
EF(X) = (X/Al)sample √∑ (X/Al)average shale
EF > 1: Enrichment (authigenic addition)
EF ‚âà 1: Detrital only
EF < 1: Depletion
